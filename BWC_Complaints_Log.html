Purpose of this log

This log records complaints received by Bethel Worldwide Corporation (BWC) from clients, counterparties or other stakeholders. As a registered agent licensed by the BVI Financial Services Commission (the "Commission"), BWC is required under the Regulatory Code 2009 to maintain a complaints handling procedure and a register of significant complaints. This log is the primary record evidencing that obligation.

What counts as a "significant complaint"?

Treat a complaint as significant where any of the following apply:

  • It alleges a breach of regulatory requirements (AML/CFT, beneficial ownership, economic substance, fitness and propriety, etc.);
  • It involves alleged loss, damage or financial detriment of a material amount;
  • It is made by a regulator, court or law enforcement agency;
  • It cannot be resolved internally within 30 calendar days of receipt;
  • It alleges misconduct or dishonesty on the part of a director, officer or employee;
  • It has the potential to materially affect BWC's reputation or licensed status.

When the Significant Complaint flag is set to Yes, the FSC notification field becomes required. The Commission expects to be notified of significant complaints promptly.

How to use this log

  1. Click + New Complaint to log an entry. The Reference No. is generated automatically as BWC-COMP-YYYY-NNNN.
  2. Acknowledge the complainant in writing within 5–7 business days and enter the Date of Acknowledgment.
  3. Update Status as the matter progresses. Once resolved, enter the Resolution Date — "Days to Resolution" is calculated automatically.
  4. Have the entry signed off in the Reviewed By field by the Compliance Officer or MLRO.

Saving your work

  • Automatic save: every change is saved immediately to this browser's local storage. You will see a green confirmation in the top-right.
  • Manual save: click 💾 Save Now for peace of mind — useful before closing the tab.
  • Backup off-device: click ⬇️ Backup JSON regularly. Local storage is browser-specific and is wiped if you clear browsing data or change device. The JSON backup is the only portable record.
  • Restore: click ⬆️ Restore JSON and select a previously exported file. This replaces the current dataset.

Exporting entries as PDF

Tick the checkbox next to each entry you want to export (or use "select all" in the table header), then choose:

  • 📄 Export PDF (Summary) — a tabular summary of the selected entries. Suitable for filing or a quick overview.
  • 📋 Export PDF (Detail) — one full page per complaint, showing every field. This is the format to use for FSC inspections or external counsel.

Retention

Records of complaints (and their resolution) should be retained for a minimum of 5 years from the date of resolution, in line with BWC's general record-retention policy under the AML/CFT Code of Practice.

Confidentiality

Complaint records may contain confidential client information and personal data. Access to this log should be restricted to authorised personnel only. Do not transmit JSON backups or PDF exports over unsecured channels.
