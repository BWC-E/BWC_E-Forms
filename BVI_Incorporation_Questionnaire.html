Company Name Guidelines:

Please provide two alternative names in case your first choice is not available, placing choices in order of preference. Some sensitive words may not be used in the name of a BVI Business Company without prior written approval by the Registry of Corporate Affairs.

The company names must end with the following words or abbreviations: Corporation, Incorporated, Societe Anonyme, Limited, Ltd., Inc., Corp.

Please include the ending in your name selection: e.g., Time International Corp. or Time Holdings Limited or Time Business Holdings Ltd.

The names which you provide below will appear exactly on the Company records.

6. Shareholders

6 / 15
A minimum of one shareholder is required, which can be an individual or a corporation.

NOTE: BVI Money Laundering legislation authorizes us to obtain and hold certified copies of the shareholder(s) passports or other photographic identification (drivers licence) and evidence of residential address. In addition, where applicable, we require additional information in the form of a professional or bank reference on behalf of shareholders as part of our procedures and policies. All identification documents are to be valid and documents evidencing proof of address should not be dated older than three (3) months.

Please provide on behalf of each Corporate Shareholder, certified or duly notarized copies of the Certificate of Incorporation, Memorandum and Articles of Association, Register of Members and Register of Directors.

Where a company listed on a stock exchange is a shareholder of a BVI Business Company, the following KYC (Know Your Client) documentation is required: Confirmation of listing, Latest audited financial statements and structure chart showing the BVI link to the listed company.

7. Register of Members

7 / 15

It is a statutory requirement that the Registers of Members be kept at the office of the Company's Registered Agent. Where a company keeps a copy of the register of members at the office of its registered agent, it shall within fifteen days of any change in the register, notify the registered agent, in writing, of the change; and provide the registered agent with a written record of the physical address of the place or places at which the original register of members is kept.

Similarly, where the place at which the original register of members is changed; the company shall provide the registered agent with the physical address of the new location of the records within fourteen days of the change of location.

Section 96 of the Business Companies Act requires that the Registered Agent in the British Virgin Islands be aware of the physical location of the Register of Members and all resolutions/minutes of the Company.

8. Directors

8 / 15

A minimum of one director is required and a company may have a corporate director.

It is a requirement of BVI law that the initial register of directors must be filed within fifteen (15) days of the appointment of the first director and any changes thereto be filed for registration at the Registry of Corporate Affairs within thirty (30) days of any changes occurring.

A letter from each director confirming his/her acceptance of appointment must accompany these forms.

9. Register of Directors

9 / 15

The information of Directors (Register of Directors) shall be kept at the office of the Registered Agent. Where a company keeps a copy of the register of directors at the office of its registered agent, it shall within fifteen (15) days of any change in the register, notify the registered agent, in writing of the change and provide the registered agent with a written record of the physical address of the place or places at which the original register of directors is kept.

Similarly, where the place at which the original register of directors is changed; the company shall provide the registered agent with the physical address of the new location of the records within fourteen (14) days of the change of location.

Section 96 of the Business Companies Act requires that the Registered Agent in the British Virgin Islands be aware of the physical location of the (Register of Directors, Company Seal, all resolutions/minutes of the Company).
